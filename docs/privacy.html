Privacy Policy

Scientific Calculator Pro  ·  Last updated: June 14, 2025

Overview

Scientific Calculator Pro is a local, on-device calculator application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information the app does and does not collect.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Calculation history

All calculations are performed entirely on your device. No calculation results or inputs are transmitted to any server.

Network Requests

The app uses the Google Fonts library to display text. On the first launch, the app may download font files from Google's servers (fonts.googleapis.com). These requests are standard HTTPS requests; no personal data is included. After the initial download, fonts are cached on your device and no further network requests are made.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party component used is Google Fonts. We do not integrate any advertising networks, analytics platforms, crash-reporting tools, or social media SDKs.

Children's Privacy

This application does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. It is a general-purpose educational tool with no user accounts or data collection.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact

Tüm hesaplamalar tamamen cihazınız üzerinde gerçekleştirilir. Hiçbir hesaplama sonucu veya girdisi herhangi bir sunucuya iletilmez.

Bu istekler standart HTTPS istekleridir; hiçbir kişisel veri içermez. İlk indirmeden sonra yazı tipleri cihazınızda önbelleğe alınır ve başka ağ isteği yapılmaz.
